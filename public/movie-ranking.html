Asma's All Time Favorite Movies

My top 3 favorite movies to date (listed in no particular order).

Coraline

The stop-motion style animation makes this a spooky masterpiece. The story, too, is rich with detail. A halloween classic in my opinion.

Boy and The Beast

A heroic story of a boy being an outcast in a strange land, but finding family in a not so friendly beast. Super cool fight sequences and scenes that will make you cry your heart out.

Vivah

This movie is just a bundle of nostalgia, comedy, romance, music, and culture. Just takes me back to when I was a kid with my sisters and cousins in grandma's room wanting to be the female lead in another universe.
